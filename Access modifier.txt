Access Modifiers:
	Allows to set the scope or accessibility or visibility of data member be it a field or class or method or constuctor. 

Default
Public
Proteted
Private. 

Default: 
	when access is not specified, it is considered as default. The scope of the default level is within the package. 

Public: 
	The particular entity is accessible throughout from within or outside the class, within or outside the package.

Protected:
	Allows access to entities through subclass of the class in which entity is declared. 

Private:
	Not accessible outside the class.
